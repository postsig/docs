# NDA Contract Configuration

Used by: **NDA** (Type 8)

NDA contracts use a specialized configuration with fields organized around confidentiality obligations and protective measures.

---

## Overview Fields

These fields appear in the contract overview section.

`term_start_date` · `term_end_date` · `cancel_date` · `execution_date` · `subscription_term` · `extended_confidentiality_period`

<Note>
NDA contracts include the **extended_confidentiality_period** field, which is unique to this contract type.
</Note>

---

## Contract Details

### Scope and Definition of Confidentiality

Defines what information is confidential and the purpose of the agreement.

`purpose` · `mutual_nda` · `confidential_information` · `permitted_use` · `term_termination`

### Obligations and Protective Measures

Outlines the obligations and protective measures for handling confidential information.

`non_use_non_disclosure` · `maintenance_of_confidentiality` · `no_obligation` · `no_license_ownership` · `exclusions_exceptions` · `non_solicitation_of_employees` · `data_disposal_tnc`

### Remedies and Legal Provisions

Specifies remedies and legal provisions for breach or disclosure.

`remedies` · `no_warranty` · `disclosure_required_by_law` · `arbitration_and_conflict_resolution` · `miscellaneous`

---

## Available Tabs

**Owner** · **Audit** · **Lineage**

<Note>
NDA contracts do not include the **DORA** tab.
</Note>
