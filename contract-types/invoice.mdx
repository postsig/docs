# Invoice Contract Configuration

Used by: **Invoice** (Type 6)

Invoice contracts use a simplified field configuration focused on basic contract details and product information.

---

## Overview Fields

These fields appear in the contract overview section.

`term_start_date` · `term_end_date` · `execution_date` · `billing_frequency` · `subscription_term` · `currency`

---

## Contract Details

### Products Licensed
`products_licensed`

### Payment Details
`payment_terms` · `renewal_period` · `cancellation_process`

### Permissions and Scope of Use
`end_users` · `number_of_users` · `market_data_types` · `data_delivery_types`

---

## Available Tabs

**Owner** · **Audit** · **Lineage**

<Note>
Invoice contracts do not include the **DORA** tab.
</Note>
